About our kitchen

Fernvale Indian Kitchen is run by a retired chef mom who loves to cook.

After nearly 20 years of cooking in a restaurant, she’s now sharing her delicious meals with neighbors, hoping they’ll enjoy them just as much as her family does.


Her speciality is cooking tasty and spicy south indian dishes.
